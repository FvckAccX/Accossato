About Me

I am Federico Accossato, I am 20 years old and I chose to do my high school education at I.T.I G. Galilei as I was attracted by the activities offered by the Computer Science and Telecommunications major. Afterwards, I would like to go to Belgium to continue my university studies in electronic engineering

Electonic & Hardware: I have deep passion for electronics and everything related to it, especially the world of hardware and how it works. I also love analyzing the weaknesses of various components and related software

Mechanic: Having a dad who owned a junkyard, from the time I got my driver's license I immediately began to get interested in cars, their components and any modifications that could be made

Travel: Having a mother who has always loved to travel, the opportunity to take me to different locations around the world sparked an interest in visiting new places and studying outside of italy non vel

Books: I love to find information on the Internet but also in textbooks, some of them related to electronics have details and illustrations that help to understand certain aspects of the components in greater detail
